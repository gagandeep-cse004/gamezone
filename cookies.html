Cookies Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2025

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit our website. They help us enhance your experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our site.

Important: We use cookies to provide you with the best possible gaming experience and to ensure our website functions properly.

Types of Cookies We Use

1. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off in our systems. They are usually only set in response to actions made by you which amount to a request for services.

  • Authentication and login status
  • Game progress and scores storage
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Shopping cart contents (if applicable)

2. Performance Cookies

These cookies allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our site.

  • Google Analytics for website traffic analysis
  • Page load times and performance metrics
  • Most popular games and pages

3. Functional Cookies

These cookies enable the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalization.

  • Remembering your game preferences
  • Language and region settings
  • Customized content recommendations

4. Advertising Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver advertisements that are relevant to you and your interests.

  • Google AdSense for personalized ads
  • Ad frequency capping
  • Ad performance measurement

Managing Your Cookies

Browser Settings

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your device and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed.

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy and services → Cookies

Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie consent banner where you can:

  • Accept all cookies for the best experience
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences

Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party services that may place cookies on your device:

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics collects information anonymously and reports website trends without identifying individual visitors.

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. AdSense uses cookies to show you ads based on your interests and your visits to our site and other sites on the Internet.

Your Choice: You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

Your Rights Regarding Cookies

Under privacy laws like GDPR and CCPA, you have the right to:

  • Know what cookies are being used
  • Control which cookies are placed on your device
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Access information about data processing

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or for other operational reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Recommendation: Please check this page periodically for any updates.
